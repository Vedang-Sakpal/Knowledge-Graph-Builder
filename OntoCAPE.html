The Dual Architecture: Balancing Power & Usability

The 5 Layers of Abstraction

OntoCAPE is built on five distinct layers, moving from the most abstract, reusable principles at the top to the most concrete, usable concepts at the bottom. This structure is key to managing complexity and promoting reuse.

Meta Layer (The Rules)
Upper Layer (General Concepts)
Conceptual Layer (Domain Knowledge)
Application-Oriented Layer (Generic Tools)
Application-Specific Layer (Your App)

The Reusability vs. Usability Trade-Off

As concepts become more specific and usable for a particular task (moving down the layers), their general reusability across different tasks decreases. OntoCAPE's architecture masterfully manages this trade-off.

Inside the Conceptual Core: The 4 Pillars of CAPE Knowledge
